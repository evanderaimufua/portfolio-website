    I'm Evander Aimufua, a dynamic software developer and recent graduate of the University of Saskatchewan with a Bachelor of Science in Applied Computing and a minor in Psychology. With a passion for both technology and human-centered design, I've led impactful initiatives as Vice President of the UX Collective, organized successful events like the university's first Design-A-Thon, and collaborated with industry partners to showcase student talent. My expertise extends to game development, where I've managed and developed projects like ENID and Origami Runner, utilizing Unity and C++ to deliver engaging experiences. Additionally, I've ventured into mobile app development with projects like CASI, employing React Native and MySQL to create innovative solutions. Proficient in languages like Python, Java, and JavaScript, and armed with tools like VScode and Git, I'm committed to crafting elegant code and intuitive interfaces that push the boundaries of what's possible in technology. Outside of coding, I'm fascinated by the intersection of technology and psychology, constantly seeking inspiration from diverse disciplines to inform my work. I'm also an avid dancer, finding joy in expressing myself through movement, and I enjoy activities like hiking, immersing myself in nature's beauty, capturing moments through music, and exploring creativity through photography.
